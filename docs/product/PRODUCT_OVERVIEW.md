# Product Overview

## What AquaCare is

AquaCare is a bilingual French and English aquaculture management app for Cameroon. It is designed for mobile use in low-connectivity environments and prioritizes offline-first workflows.

## Primary users

- Fish farmers.
- Farm managers.
- Support and operations staff who need production visibility.

## Product principles

- The mobile app must work when connectivity is unstable.
- The backend is the source of truth for business outcomes.
- The UI must stay bilingual.
- UUID-based offline creation must be preserved.

## Main domains

- Accounts and farm setup.
- Aquaculture production cycles and logs.
- Commerce and feed-related purchasing.
- Notifications and reminders.
- Support and communication flows.
